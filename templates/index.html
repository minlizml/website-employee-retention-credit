Get paid back for KEEPING EMPLOYEES

The Employee Retention Credit (ERC) is a refundable tax credit for businesses that kept employees on payroll during the COVID-19 pandemic.
Eligible businesses can receive up to $26,000 per employee across 2020 and 2021.
